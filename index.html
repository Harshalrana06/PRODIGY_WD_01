About OpenAI

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company. Our mission is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI)—highly autonomous systems that outperform humans at most economically valuable work—benefits all of humanity.

Key Projects

Some of OpenAI’s key projects include ChatGPT, DALL·E for image generation, and Codex for coding assistance. These tools have redefined interaction with machines using natural language and visual prompts.

Global Impact

OpenAI's models are used in education, healthcare, customer service, coding, design, and more. By providing accessible tools, OpenAI empowers developers, creators, and businesses worldwide.
